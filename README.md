# Marketing Glossary

A modern **Marketing Glossary Website** that explains popular marketing terms like **KOL, KOC, KOS, ROI, CAC, CTR, Conversion Rate**, and more.

This project aims to build an **open marketing terminology database** for marketers, product managers, founders, and students who want to quickly understand marketing concepts.

🔗 Live Website
https://zanderxxx.github.io/marketing-glossary/

---

## What is this project

Marketing Glossary is a lightweight and continuously growing collection of marketing terms and definitions.

It helps people quickly understand:

* Influencer marketing terms (KOL / KOC / KOS)
* Growth marketing concepts
* Digital marketing metrics
* Advertising terminology
* Product and user acquisition vocabulary

This site is designed to be:

* Simple
* Fast
* SEO-friendly
* Easy to expand
